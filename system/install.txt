How to install:
1)Copy the database to your mysql
2)change the connectivity variables on the includes\server.php file
  and on the admin\admin\users\connection.php to the configuration of
  your computer

How to use:
1)log in. if you dont have an account register by clicking on registe    on the login page.
2) Once logged in, click on Chat to start a session with the Chatbot
3)Logout once done.
